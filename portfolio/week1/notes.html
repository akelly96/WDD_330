Week 1 Notes and Questions

Notes

  • Design for mobile devices first.
  • By creating desktop first, you don't account for touch interaction.
  • Start small and scale up.
  • The less steps it takes to complete an action the better.
  • Make the most common button in reach distance for mobile devices.
  • Menus should be at the bottom of mobile devices
  • Some people still do not recognize the hamburger button as the menu button. Use the word menu instead.
  • Buttons should be big enough to click on in mobile devices
  • The number pad, not the keyboard, should be used for number inputs
  • Do not use pop-up ads
  • Ads should be simple and not overpowering.

Questions:

  1. When would one ever use local storage?
  2. How long can local storage store a variable? Where does it store it?
